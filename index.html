Thiruvalluvar Statue

The statue is built as per Indian architectural styles, and is hollow inside. However, tourists are allowed to climb only up to the foot of the statue. Along with the Vivekananda Rock Memorial, the statue attracts millions of tourists annually and is one of the must-visit spots in your trip to Tamil Nadu.
